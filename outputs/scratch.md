Here are 10 simple options for a brunch potluck:

1. **Mini Quiches**: Offer a variety of fillings like spinach and cheese or bacon and mushroom.

2. **Fruit Salad**: A mix of seasonal fruits, perhaps with a honey-lime dressing.

3. **Bagel and Lox Platter**: Include cream cheese, sliced tomatoes, onions, and capers.

4. **Pancake Bites**: Small pancake balls served with maple syrup for dipping.

5. **Avocado Toast Bar**: Provide avocado mash, toast, and toppings like tomatoes, radishes, and feta.

6. **Breakfast Casserole**: Layers of eggs, sausage, cheese, and hashbrowns baked to perfection.

7. **Yogurt Parfait Station**: Greek yogurt with granola, berries, and nuts for toppings.

8. **Scones with Jam and Cream**: Serve a variety like blueberry or cranberry.

9. **Caprese Salad Skewers**: Cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil with a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

10. **Muffins**: A selection including blueberry, banana nut, and chocolate chip.

These options are easy to prepare, serve, and will cater to a range of tastes and dietary preferences.
